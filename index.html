My Data Visualization Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio of data visualizations. Here, I present a selection of charts that reflect my approach to turning raw data into meaningful stories. Each visualization is accompanied by a narrative that explains its context, purpose, and the insights it reveals.

India’s Telecom Story: Growth, Pricing and the 5G Shift
This segment captures major trends shaping India’s telecom sector—from competitive dynamics and market consolidation to tariff changes and data usage growth driven by 5G. These charts explore how customer behavior and corporate strategy have evolved post-Jio's entry and in the wake of new technological rollouts.
Market Share of Indian Telcos (2023)
This donut chart visualizes the wireless subscriber base distribution among India's major telecom players as of 2023. Jio continues to lead, followed by Airtel and Vi. The chart shows how consolidation and customer churn have reshaped market dynamics.
Tools Used:
Chart.js, HTML, TRAI Data
Tariff Revisions by Telcos Over Time
This line graph tracks key tariff hikes by India’s major telcos since Jio’s market entry. It highlights three major rounds of price revisions that reset the baseline for ARPU (Average Revenue Per User) in the industry.
Tools Used:
Illustrator, Company Filings
Mobile Data Usage After 5G Rollout
This chart shows quarterly mobile data consumption in India, highlighting the surge after the 5G launch. Jio and Airtel dominate data usage, reflecting customer uptake and expanding network coverage.
Tools Used:
Excel, Illustrator
