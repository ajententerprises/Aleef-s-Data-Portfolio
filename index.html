India’s Telecom Story: Growth, Pricing and the 5G Shift

Market Share of Indian Telcos (2023)
Explaining the Market Share Chart
This area chart visualizes the shifting market share among India's major telecom operators since the Vodafone-Idea merger in 2018. It reveals how Jio and Airtel have steadily captured more subscribers, while Vodafone Idea (Vi) has seen a significant drop in its user base. The chart underscores the impact of aggressive pricing strategies, network investments, and industry consolidation on subscriber dynamics. Ultimately, it highlights the competitive pressures and consumer choices that continue to reshape India's telecom landscape.
Tools Used:
Chart.js, HTML, TRAI Data

Vaccine Inequality: How Rich Nations Raced Ahead in the Global Jab Drive
As COVID-19 vaccines began rolling out in early 2021, a striking gap emerged between wealthier nations and the rest of the world. While countries like the US, UK, and Israel rapidly immunised large parts of their population, many low-income nations were left behind—waiting for donations, approvals, or supplies. This segment visualises how the global vaccination effort unfolded in the first half of 2021, revealing stark disparities in access and pace.
A Race Against Time—and Inequality
This animated bar chart race tracks the percentage of population fully vaccinated in various countries until July 2021. It highlights how high-income countries surged ahead early, leaving others behind. The widening gap reflects the systemic inequality in global health access.

Flourish
