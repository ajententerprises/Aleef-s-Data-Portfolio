My Data Visualization Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio of data visualizations. Here, I present a selection of charts that reflect my approach to turning raw data into meaningful stories. Each visualization is accompanied by a narrative that explains its context, purpose, and the insights it reveals.

India’s Telecom Story: Growth, Pricing and the 5G Shift
This segment captures major trends shaping India’s telecom sector—from competitive dynamics and market consolidation to tariff changes and data usage growth driven by 5G. These charts explore how customer behavior and corporate strategy have evolved post-Jio's entry and in the wake of new technological rollouts.
How India’s Telecom Sector Consolidated Since 2016
This area chart traces the transformation of India's telecom landscape, beginning with the intense disruption caused by Jio’s entry in 2016. Within a few years, the market consolidated around three major players—Jio, Airtel, and Vi—as smaller firms exited or merged. The consolidation not only reshaped competition but also influenced pricing and investment patterns.
TOOLS USED:
Chart.js, custom annotations, HTML/CSS
Mobile Tariff Trends Since Jio's Entry
This line chart shows how prepaid mobile recharge prices in India have changed since 2018. The story begins with Jio’s entry in 2016, which disrupted the market by offering ultra-cheap data plans. Older telecom companies had to slash their own prices to stay competitive, leading to a period of intense price wars and declining revenues. By late 2019, however, the remaining players—Jio, Airtel, and Vi—began hiking tariffs in a coordinated manner to boost profitability.
TOOLS USED:
Google Sheets, Adobe Illustrator
India's Soaring Mobile Data Usage
The bar chart shows the explosion in mobile data consumption in India from 2016 to 2023. Jio’s free and cheap data kickstarted a data revolution, making India the world’s largest consumer of mobile data. Even with rising tariffs, per-user data consumption has continued to climb—partly due to video-heavy platforms like YouTube and Instagram and the rollout of 5G in select cities.
TOOLS USED:
Datawrapper, custom annotations
5G Rollout: Where Each Telco Stands
This stacked column chart shows the 5G rollout status across different telecom circles in India. Airtel’s approach has focused on major cities first, while Jio’s standalone 5G is being pushed to more areas. Vodafone Idea, constrained by debt, lags behind significantly, highlighting how financial health directly impacts technology adoption.
TOOLS USED:
Adobe Illustrator, TRAI dataset
A Race Against Time—and Inequality
This animated bar chart race tracks the percentage of population fully vaccinated in various countries until July 2021. It highlights how high-income countries surged ahead early, leaving others behind. The widening gap reflects the systemic inequality in global health access.
